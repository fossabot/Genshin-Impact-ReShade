* !! * READ BEFORE INSTALLING * !! *

1. IMPORTANT
- Do not share screenshots of the game that display your UID, visible shaders, or ReShade overlay.
- If you plan to record a video for YouTube or any other platform, please conceal or censor your UID and username.
- Ensure you're using the latest operating system build, update your graphics card drivers, and chipset drivers to achieve optimal performance.

2. CAN I USE MY CUSTOM PRESETS IN STELLA MOD?
Yes, of course. You can place your custom presets in Stella Mod Resources.

3. BENEFITS
If you're interested in trying out the Ray Tracing feature (beta version) and obtaining other benefits in Genshin Impact, consider becoming a Stella Mod Plus subscriber.
Nvidia RTX graphics cards are not required.

4. CAN I ADD MY PRESETS TO STELLA MOD?
Absolutely yes! We welcome community contributions and invite you to submit a new Pull Request on our GitHub repository at:
